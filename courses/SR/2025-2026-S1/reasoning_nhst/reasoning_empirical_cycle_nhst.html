Empirical Cycle

By Adriaan de Groot

The components

  • Observation
    • Idea for hypothesis
  • Induction
    • General rule
    • Hypothesis
  • Deduction
    • Expectation / Prediction
    • Operationalization
  • Testing
    • Test hypothesis
    • Compare data to prediction
  • Evaluation
    • Interpret results in terms of hypothesis
